About

Vidarbha Youth Welfare Society, Amravati.

Vidarbha Youth Welfare Society was established by the great visionary of this region Prof Ram eghe in the year 1965, with a clear vision of making it a world class premier organization for primary, secondary, higher, technical, and professional education. With this vision the members of this society dedicated themselves and continued to establish and promote high quality technical education and research facilities.

The college has an excellent academic ambience with a calm and peaceful environment for teaching-learning process. We believe in simplicity with quality. We do not make sky promises. We understand ground realities. And with this understanding we plan our activities. In an educational set up we know and understand the expectations and requirements of various stake holders: be it parents, students, faculty, industry, society and the nation as the ultimate. Our endeavor is always to come up to the expectations of all the stakeholders and we keep striving for the same at PRM-CEAM as well.
